FUN Tickets

FUN Tickets

FUN Tickets are $3 each or purchase a strip of 4 for $9 (Save $3)

Available for purchase at: Admissions, Junction Plaza and Air Hike Ticket Boots

Daily: April - October

Can be used at: Endangered Species Carousel, Kaleidescope Butterfly Garden, Expedition Africa A Virtual Reality Experience, Acacia Station Giraffe Deck and Treehouse Trek. Each attraction requires one FUN Ticket per entry per person.

FUN Tickets are not accepted for the Air Hike and Zoofari attractions
